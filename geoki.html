GEOKINETICS

GEOKINETICS IS ONE OF THE LARGEST INDEPENDENT, INTERNATIONAL LAND AND SHALLOW WATER GEOPHYSICAL SERVICE COMPANIES OFFERING A BROAD RANGE OF SPECIALIZED GEOPHYSICAL SOLUTIONS TO THE PETROLEUM AND MINING INDUS- TRIES, WORLDWIDE.

PT Oikos Perkasa (“POP”) is the agent company engaging as Local Partner of “Geokinetics” for Indonesia. Geokinetics Inc., is a leading geophysical service company offering a broad range of specialized geophysical services to the petroleum and mining industries, worldwide. Its services include land, shallow water OBC (ocean bottom cable) and TZ (transition zone) seismic data acquisition and advanced processing and interpretation services. The company has years of experience working in areas of diverse culture, extreme environmental sensitivity and areas with industrial variables.

We have a clear understanding of these issues and how they contribute to some of the specific challenges and complexities of the projects we undertake . Geokinetics has the capacity to operate in almost any environment; whether arctic tundra, desert, open grasslands or plains, high mountains with rough terrain, foothills, dense jungles, thick forest, swamps, marshes, mangrove or in the area we reference as the transition zone (between land and deep marine).

We use the most appropriate acquisition methodology and equipment for such oper- ations. We cover an operating range from high land all the way through to ocean bottom cable marine operations (up to 100 meters, approximately 300 feet) water depth.
